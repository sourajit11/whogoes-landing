Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. What We Do

WhoGoes ("we", "us") operates whogoes.co and app.whogoes.co. We provide event attendee data sourced from publicly available LinkedIn posts. Our platform helps B2B sales and marketing teams identify and reach people who have publicly announced their attendance at trade shows, conferences, and events.

2. Data We Collect

From our customers (you):

  • Account information: name, email address, password
  • Payment information: processed securely by our payment provider (we do not store card details)
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, credits consumed

Event attendee data (contacts in our database):

  • Name, job title, company name
  • Work email address (verified via third-party services)
  • LinkedIn profile URL
  • Source LinkedIn post URL (the public post indicating event attendance)
  • Company firmographic data (industry, size, location)

3. Data Sources

All attendee data is sourced from publicly available LinkedIn posts where individuals have voluntarily shared their attendance at events. We do not access private profiles, use login credentials, or scrape behind authentication walls. Contact enrichment (email, company details) is performed using third-party data providers.

4. How We Use Data

  • To provide our service: delivering event attendee lists to customers
  • To verify email accuracy and maintain data quality
  • To process payments and manage accounts
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To communicate service updates and support

5. Data Sharing

We share attendee data with our paying customers for legitimate B2B outreach purposes. We do not sell customer account data to third parties. We use third-party services for payment processing, email verification, and analytics.

6. Data Removal & Opt-Out

If you are listed in our database and want your information removed, email us at hello@whogoes.co with the subject line "Data Removal Request." We will process your request within 72 hours.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. No advertising cookies are used.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and access controls. Customer passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@whogoes.co.
